Problem → Solution → Outcome

A product story built for gym owners, SaaS founders, and engineering leaders evaluating scalable mobile platforms.

Problem

Every gym wanted its own app — but custom builds don't scale

Fitness and coaching businesses need branded apps with memberships, class booking, programs, community, messaging, and payments. Building a separate codebase per client is slow, costly, and impossible to maintain at scale.

Solution

One white-label platform, infinite branded experiences

I designed a multi-tenant architecture with Flutter mobile apps (iOS & Android), a Svelte web admin, and Firebase/GCP backend — plus automated CI/CD to ship each client's branded app without rebuilding from scratch.

Outcome

30+ client apps live, repeatable delivery at scale

FBP UK now onboard new gym and coaching brands with consistent product quality, Stripe-powered monetization, and a proven deployment pipeline — turning a business vision into production apps that members actually use.
